And.. this is my hometown!

Bangil is a sub-district that serves as the capital of Pasuruan Regency, East Java. Currently, it is nicknamed Bangkodir or Bangil the Embroidery City. Bangil is also known as Bangil the Santri City due to the many Islamic boarding schools in the area. The city of Bangil is densely populated with various ethnic groups, including Javanese, Chinese, Arab, Banjar, Madurese, Balinese, and others. They live side by side and respect each other.
